Programs Offered

MBA Programme

Duration: 2 Years

At ABBS, our MBA programme is designed to shape confident and future-ready leaders. As one of the leading AICTE-approved and NAAC 'A' Grade institutions in Bangalore, we combine academic rigour with strong industry integration to ensure students graduate career-ready.

Our MBA goes beyond classroom learning. Through internships, live projects, corporate interactions, industry visits and case-based learning, students gain hands-on exposure to real business challenges. With dual specialisation options, global immersion opportunities and value-added certifications, we prepare graduates to thrive in dynamic corporate environments.

Backed by experienced faculty from academia and industry, students receive personalised mentoring and strategic guidance throughout their journey. Our strong placement record, with leading MNCs, startups and reputed organisations recruiting regularly, reflects our commitment to career outcomes.

Eligibility: Candidates must hold a recognised graduate degree (10+2+3/4 pattern) with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST/Cat I).

PGDM Programme

Duration: 2 Years

At ABBS School of Management, the two-year AICTE-approved PGDM programme is designed to develop future business leaders through industry-focused learning. Ranked 21st among Outstanding B-Schools of Excellence (CSR-GHRDC) and among the top PGDM colleges in India, ABBS offers a dynamic, industry-aligned curriculum.

Students gain hands-on exposure through live projects, case studies, internships, business simulations, and corporate interactions. The programme also features global immersion opportunities, specialised labs like Blockchain and Investment Labs, research incubation, and six value-added certifications.

With experienced faculty, strong alumni networks, corporate mentoring, and diverse specialisations including Business Analytics, ABBS ensures graduates develop leadership, analytical thinking, and career-ready skills for today’s competitive business environment.
